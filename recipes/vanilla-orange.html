Vanilla Orange Smoothie

Description

A delicious and refreshing detox drink that removes toxins from your body with creamy vanilla orange flavor!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup or 120ml of almond milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon or 1.2ml of vanilla extract
  • 1 medium orange
  • 1/2 of a banana
  • 1 cup or 140ml of frozen mango
  • 3-4 ice cubes

Nutritional Information

  • Calories 224
  • Fat 2g
  • Saturated fat 0g
  • Carbohydrates 52g
  • Fiber 6g
  • Sugar 41g
  • Protein 3g
  • Sodium 80mg
