Watermelon Rosemary Smoothie

Description

A refreshing smoothie packed with antioxidants which can help prevent cancer with a Rosemary twist!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup or 140g of seedless watermelon
  • 1 medium orange
  • 3/4 teaspoon or 1g of rosemary
  • 1 cup or 135g of frozen strawberries

Nutritional Information

  • Calories 159
  • Fat 0g
  • Saturated fat 0g
  • Carbohydrates 40g
  • Fiber 7g
  • Sugar 31g
  • Protein 2g
  • Sodium 2mg
