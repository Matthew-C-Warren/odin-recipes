Ginger Cucumber Smoothie

Description

A refreshing smoothie packed with antioxidants which can help prevent cancer

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup or 140 ml of coconut water
  • 1 cup of 120 g of sliced cucumber
  • 1 tablespoon or 15 ml of honey
  • 1 cup or 135g of strawberries
  • 1 teaspoon or 2g of ginger

Nutritional Information

  • Calories 197
  • Fat 1g
  • Saturated fat 0g
  • Fiber 8g
  • Sugar 40g
  • Protein 1g
  • Sodium 193mg
